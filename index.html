EN — Summary & positioning

Category-level .com for living processors

LivingProcessor.com is a descriptive, deeptech domain designed as a category name for the coming wave of biological processors, organoid intelligence and hybrid wetware/silicon compute.

This asset is aimed at CTOs, Heads of R&D, Emerging Tech leaders, cloud & deeptech funds who want to:

  • Label living processors / biological processing units in plain language;
  • Build an ingredient brand (“Living Processor Inside”, etc.);
  • Or host a neutral industry hub for biocomputing and living compute.

This website does not sell any technology, product, platform, patent or biological material — only the livingprocessor.com domain name.

C-level & CFO angle

  • Descriptive wording that reduces category education cost vs. invented names.
  • Single, scarce .com that can support long-term narrative & ecosystem.
  • Optional, asymmetric bet on biocomputing becoming a meaningful share of future workloads.
